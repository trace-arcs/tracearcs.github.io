Super PAC Scramble

Jacob Greene & Melissa Bianchi

Project Description

Super PAC Scramble is a single-player augmented reality game that educates players about monetary donations for the 2016 U.S. presidential election. Using campaign logos as trigger images, the game invites players to select one of seven presidential frontrunners and collect financial support for his or her campaign. By digitally dragging and dropping names and donations onto a candidateâ€™s logo, players learn which presidential contenders are and are not supported by super PACs as well as the difficulties of becoming a viable candidate through small, individual donations.
